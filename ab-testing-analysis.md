# A/B Experimentation & Incentive Optimization

## Problem
Evaluated the ROI of a new incentive feature across a dataset of 20,000+ users to determine its impact on conversion rates.

## Action
- Compared control and treatment groups in an A/B experiment
- Performed statistical hypothesis testing using SQL and t-tests
- Calculated p-values and confidence intervals at a 95% confidence level

## Impact
- Identified a 4.6% statistically significant uplift in conversion rate (p < 0.05)
- Supported full rollout of the feature projected to increase monthly recurring revenue (MRR) by 6–8%

## Tools Used
SQL, A/B Testing, Hypothesis Testing, Statistical Analysis, Product Analytics
