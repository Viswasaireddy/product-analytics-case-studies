# Multicity Unit Economics & Margin Optimization

## Problem
Diagnosed declining profitability across 100k+ marketplace transactions. Identified 3 cities operating below contribution margin breakeven due to high delivery costs and 12% discount leakage.

## Action
- Built SQL revenue–cost models
- Created Excel dashboards to track AOV, CAC, and Gross Margin
- Performed city-level variance analysis to identify cost drivers

## Impact
- Improved contribution margin by 14%
- Reduced variable costs by 9%
- Identified ₹22L annual revenue optimization opportunity

## Tools Used
SQL, Excel, Data Analysis, Unit Economics
